# Privacy Policy for max-w

_Last updated: 2026-07-07_

max-w does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

## What the extension does

- **Runs locally in your browser.** All logic executes on the page you are viewing.
- **Makes no network requests.** The extension never sends data anywhere.
- **Stores only your own settings.** Your per-site preferences (whether max-w is
  enabled for a site, the chosen width, and any element you picked) are saved using
  Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync`. This data stays within your Google account's
  Chrome sync and is used solely to remember your settings across sessions and devices.

## What the extension does NOT do

- No analytics or tracking.
- No cookies.
- No reading, collecting, or transmitting of page content, browsing history, or
  personal information.
- No third-party services.

## Permissions

- `storage` — save and sync your per-site settings.
- `activeTab` — let the popup apply changes to the tab you are currently viewing.
- Host access (`<all_urls>`) — required so the extension can adjust the layout of any
  site you choose to enable it on. No site data is read or sent.
